# Three-Point Box

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Box' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a rectangular box by specifying three points on its base and its height.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-box" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click one corner of the base.
3. Click another point on the base.
4. Move the cursor to adjust the size of the base, and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Command Dialog</Kst>
      - **Select target bodies:**
         - Functions the same as the Boolean command. Refer to [**Boolean**](../solid/boolean) command for details.
      - **Width:** Specify the width.
      - **Length:** Specify the length.
      - **Height:** Specify the height.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
5. Move the cursor to adjust the height, and click to confirm.
7. The rectangular box is created.
