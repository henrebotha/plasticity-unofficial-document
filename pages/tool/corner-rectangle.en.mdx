# Corner Rectangle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Corner Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

Create a rectangle by specifying two points on the diagonal. Length can be set using mouse actions or numerically.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-corner-rectangle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the start point on the diagonal of the rectangle.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specify length with a numerical value](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Use Knife Mode.](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode) 
   - <kbd>C</kbd> **Center / Corner:** Switch to Center Rectangle.
   - <kbd>S</kbd> **Square:** Limits to a square.
3. Click at the endpoint on the diagonal.
4. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
5. A rectangle is created.
