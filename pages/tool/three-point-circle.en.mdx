# Three-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

Create a circle specified by three points on the circumference.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click on the first point on the circumference.
3. Move the cursor and click on the second point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specify the diameter with a numerical value](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Use Knife Mode.](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode) 
   - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrain to the X, Y, or Z-axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Move the cursor and click on the third point on the circumference.
5. A circle is created.
