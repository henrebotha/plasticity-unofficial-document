# Three-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a circle by specifying three points on its circumference.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-circle" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click a point on the circumference of the circle.
3. Move the cursor and click a second point on the circumference.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Enables direct input of dimensions. See [here](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically) for details.
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Refer to [Using Knife Mode](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrains to X, Y, Z axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Move the cursor to adjust the size, and click to confirm.
5. The circle is created.
