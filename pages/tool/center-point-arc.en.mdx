# Center Point Arc

<Reference shortcut='' command='Center Point Arc' button='Toolbar' />

Create an arc by specifying the center and two points on the circumference.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-center-point-arc" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the circle's center.
3. Move the cursor and click on the first point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis:** [Constrain to the X, Y, or Z-axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Move the cursor and click on the second point on the circumference.
5. An arc is created.
