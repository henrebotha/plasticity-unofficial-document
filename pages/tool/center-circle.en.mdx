# Center Circle 

<Reference shortcut="" command="Center Circle" button="Toolbar" />

Create a circle by specifying the center and the diameter. Optionally, adjust the angle relative to the construction plane.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-center-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the circle's center.
3. Move the cursor and click on a point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specify the diameter with a numerical value](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Use Knife Mode.](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode) 
   - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical/Horizontal:** Change the inclination with respect to the construction plane.
   - <kbd>X</kbd>/<kbd>Y</kbd> **X/Y-axis:** [Constrain to the X or Y-axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
5. A circle is created.
