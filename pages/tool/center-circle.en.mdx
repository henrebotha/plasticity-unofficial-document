# Center Circle 

<Reference shortcut="" command="Center Circle" button="Toolbar" />

You can create a circle by specifying the center and diameter. 

## Basic Usage

1. Click the <ButtonIcon type="button-center-circle" /> button on the toolbar.
2. Click at the center of the circle.
3. Move the cursor to adjust the size of the circle and click to confirm.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
      - <kbd>Tab</kbd>: Allows direct input of dimensions. For details, see [here](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
      - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Please refer to [Using Knife Mode](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode).
      - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical/Horizontal:** Changes the tilt relative to the construction plane.
      - <kbd>X</kbd>/<kbd>Y</kbd> **X/Y-axis:** [Constrain to X, Y axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
4. Click OK in the command dialog or right-click to confirm.
5. The circle is created.
