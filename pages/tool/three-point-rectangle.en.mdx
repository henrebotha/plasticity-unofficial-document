# Three Point Rectangle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three Point Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

Create a rectangle by specifying three points. Length can be set using mouse actions or with a numerical value.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-rectangle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the start point of one side of the rectangle.
   - <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> : [Specify length a numerical value](./sketching-essentials#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife** : [Use Knife Mode.](./sketching-essentials#use-knife-mode) 
   - <kbd>X</kbd>/ <kbd>Y</kbd>/<kbd>Z</kbd> **X/Y/Z-axis** : [Constrain to the X, Y, or Z-axis](./sketching-essentials#constrain-to-an-axis).
3. Click at the endpoint of the same side of the rectangle.
4. Click at the third point of the rectangle.
5. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
6. A rectangle is created.
