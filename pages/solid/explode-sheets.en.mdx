# Explode Shells [#explode-sheets]

<Reference shortcut="" command="Explode Shells" button="" />

Separate all Faces from selected Solids or Sheets into individual Faces.

1. Select the Solids or Sheets you want to separate.
2. Press <kbd>Alt + J</kbd>.
3. The objects are separated.
