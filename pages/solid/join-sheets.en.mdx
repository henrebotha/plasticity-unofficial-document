# Join Sheets

<Reference shortcut="J" command="Join Sheets" button="" />

Join Sheets with matching Edges.

## Basic Usage

1. Select the Sheets you want to join.
2. Press <kbd>J</kbd>.
3. Sheets are joined.

<Callout emoji="❗">
**Note**<br />
To join two Sheets, their Edges must be perfectly aligned.
</Callout>
