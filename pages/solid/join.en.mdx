# Join

<Reference shortcut="J" command="Join" button="" />

This command automatically calls the following command depending on the selected object.
    - When Curves are selected: [**Join Curves**](../sketch/join#join-Curves) in the Sketch Commands section.
    - When Sheets are selected: see **Join Sheets** described below.

## Join Sheets

You can join Sheets with matching Edges.

1. Select the Sheets you want to join.
2. Press <kbd>J</kbd>.
3. Sheets are joined.

<Callout emoji="❗">
**Note**<br />
To join two Sheets, their Edges must be perfectly aligned.
</Callout>
