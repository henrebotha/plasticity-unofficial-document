# Join

<Reference shortcut="J" command="Join" button="" />

This command automatically calls the following command depending on the selected object.
    - When Curves are selected: [**Join Curves**](#join-Curves) described below.
    - When Sheets are selected: see [**Join Sheets**](../solid/join#join-sheets) in the Solid Commands section.

## Join Curves

You can join Curves at connecting points.

1. Select the Curves you want to join.
2. Press <kbd>J</kbd>.
3. Curves are joined.

<Callout emoji="❗">
**Note**<br />
To join two Curves, their endpoints must be perfectly aligned.
When they are perfectly aligned, the Points will turn blue-green. If they appear purple, they are not aligned. To ensure a precise alignment, see [**Object Snaps**](../plasticity-essentials/grid-and-snap#object-snap).
</Callout>
